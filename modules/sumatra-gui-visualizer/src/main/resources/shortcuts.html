Left mouse click

ctrl: Look at Ball
shift: Kick to
ctrl+shift: Follow mouse
alt: ruler

Right mouse click
(Simulation Only)

none: place ball
ctrl: 8m/s to target
shift: stop at target
ctrl+shift: 2m/s at target
above combinations + alt: chip ball

Ruler

ctrl or alt + left mouse drag

Bot selection

ctrl + left mouse drag: select bots
ctrl + right mouse drag: send selected bots to line
